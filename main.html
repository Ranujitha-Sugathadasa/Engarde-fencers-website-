WHO ARE WE

Engard fencers is a multinational fencing club founded in 1998. For 2023 we celebrate our 25th anniversary. Our main target is to find talented fencers around the world and train them to become world champions. our motto is to unleash your inner athlete. we are proud to say that we helped many fencers to win an Olympic medal.
